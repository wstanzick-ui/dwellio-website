How it works

From the box to your pocket
in seconds

No more paper manuals, forgotten warranty cards, or phone trees. Dwellio turns every product you own into a smart, searchable, AI-ready record.

1
Add your product
Scan the QR code on the box, enter a model number, or search by brand. Your product is added to your library instantly with its manual, warranty, and maintenance schedule pre-loaded.
2
Ask anything
Dwellio AI is trained only on your exact model's documentation. Ask about setup, troubleshooting, cleaning, or warranty — and get a precise answer in seconds, not hours.
3
Stay on top of maintenance
Dwellio tracks maintenance schedules for every product in your library and sends you reminders before things break — not after.
4
Order parts, schedule service
When something needs fixing, Dwellio finds the exact part for your model or connects you to a qualified technician — no guesswork, no wrong parts.
